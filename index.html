Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How does the 14-day free trial work?

Our 14-day trial gives you full access to all features of your selected plan. No credit card is required to start. At the end of your trial, you can choose to subscribe or your account will automatically downgrade to our free plan with limited features.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard. When upgrading, the new plan takes effect immediately. When downgrading, the new plan takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Is there a setup fee?

No, we don't charge any setup or hidden fees. You only pay for your subscription plan, and what you see is what you get.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and for annual plans, we can also accommodate bank transfers.

How secure is my data?

We take security very seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard protocols and regularly undergo security audits. Your data is backed up daily and we are GDPR compliant.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. If you cancel, you'll still have access to your paid features until the end of your billing period.
